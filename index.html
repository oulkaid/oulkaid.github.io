Thesis

Since October 2022, I am conducting a PhD thesis at LIP, Verimag, and Aniah, on formal models of integrated circuits for transistor level electrical verification, under the supervision of Matthieu Moy, Pascal Raymond, Bruno Ferres, and Mehdi Khosravian.
